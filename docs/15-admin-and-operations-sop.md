## Current SOP Outline

### Report Review

- open reports queue
- inspect image, location, severity, and metadata
- check for fraud or moderation indicators
- decide next workflow action

### Status Management

- review current status
- update status through dashboard/admin workflows
- ensure status changes are traceable

### Fraud Handling

- inspect suspicious reports
- review duplicate or abuse indicators
- escalate or restrict where applicable

### Officer / SLA Monitoring

- inspect ward / officer-related operational views
- review overdue or SLA-sensitive items
